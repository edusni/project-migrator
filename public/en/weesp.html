Weesp

Historic charm at the gates of Amsterdam

About Weesp

Weesp is a historic town that was officially incorporated into Amsterdam in 2022, becoming the capital's newest district. Despite the administrative merger, Weesp maintains its unique character as a small Dutch town, with picturesque canals, historic architecture, and a peaceful atmosphere that contrasts with the bustle of central Amsterdam.

The town dates back to the 13th century and has a rich cultural heritage, including windmills, historic churches, and the famous Muiden Castle nearby.

Main Attractions

  • Historic Center - Medieval streets with 17th-century houses
  • Grote Kerk - 15th-century Gothic church
  • Weesp Canals - Picturesque canals for walks
  • Muiderslot - Medieval castle just a few kilometers away
  • De Vriendschap Windmill - Historic windmill
  • Weesp Museum - Local history and Weesp ceramics

Weesp Porcelain

Weesp was historically famous for its porcelain production in the 18th century. "Weesper Porselein" was known for its exceptional quality and today is a collector's item. The local museum displays original pieces from this artisanal tradition.

Practical Information

How to Get There

Weesp is easily accessible from Amsterdam:

  • Train - 15 minutes from Amsterdam Centraal (NS Sprinter)
  • Bicycle - Approximately 45 minutes from center
  • Car - 20 minutes via A1
  • Bus - Regional lines connect to Bijlmer and Amstel

Who Is It For?

  • Travelers seeking Dutch authenticity
  • History and architecture lovers
  • Those wanting to escape tourist crowds
  • Cyclists exploring Amsterdam's surroundings
  • Visitors to Muiden Castle

Tips

  • Combine your visit with Muiden Castle (Muiderslot), just 4km away
  • The weekly market takes place on Wednesdays
  • Rent a bicycle to explore the Vecht region
  • Visit in summer to enjoy the canalside terraces
  • The train station is a 5-minute walk from the historic center

Combined Day Trips

Weesp is perfect for a half-day trip combined with:

  • Muiderslot - Must-see medieval castle
  • Naarden - Star-shaped fortified town
  • Vecht River - Scenic route with historic mansions
