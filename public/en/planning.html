Documents Required

Good news for most travelers: No visa required for tourist stays up to 90 days for EU, US, UK, Brazilian, and many other nationalities.

  • Valid Passport: Must be valid for at least 3 months AFTER your return date
  • Accommodation Proof: Hotel booking, Airbnb confirmation, or invitation letter
  • Financial Proof: Bank statement or credit card (they may ask at immigration)
  • Return Ticket: Proof you're leaving the Schengen Area within 90 days
  • Travel Insurance: Highly recommended, especially for healthcare
ETIAS (2025+): From 2025, non-EU visitors need ETIAS authorization (‚Ç¨7, valid 3 years). Apply online before traveling.
