Why "Brutally Honest"?

Most travel guides tell you what you want to hear. Amsterdam is magical! Everything is beautiful! Everyone is nice!

The truth is more nuanced. Amsterdam has real problems: overtourism, high costs, strict regulations, and cultural norms that can surprise visitors. I believe you deserve to know all of this before you arrive.

When I tell you something is expensive, overrated, or not worth your time — it's because I want you to have the best experience possible, not just click on affiliate links.
