Public Transport (GVB)

The backbone of Amsterdam transport. 15 lines covering the city. Most useful: lines 2, 5, 13, 17 connect major attractions.

Tip: Enter through any door, tap your card at the reader. Don't forget to check out when leaving!

Transport Apps

  • 9292: Official Dutch public transport planner. Best for routes and times.
  • GVB: Buy tickets, see real-time arrivals. Essential for tram/metro.
  • NS: Dutch railways. For trains to Schiphol, day trips.
  • Uber/Bolt: Ride-hailing. Cheaper than taxis.
  • Donkey Republic: Bike sharing. Unlock via app, return at any station.

Common Transport Mistakes

  • Not checking out: Tap card when entering AND leaving. Fines are ‚Ç¨50+.
  • Walking in bike lanes: Red pavement = bikes only. Locals won't stop!
  • Buying single tickets: Day passes are cheaper if taking 3+ trips.
  • Taxis at Central Station: Often overcharge tourists. Use apps instead.
  • Renting bikes at tourist traps: Walk 5 minutes from Central for better prices.
